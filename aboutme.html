Deployment Guides

How I would personally utilize the MDS tools

Ghost Gateway

Type into the secure sidebar. Our engine hashes PII locally before sending it to the LLM. Click 'Restore' to swap hashes back to real text on your machine.

  • > Sending Sensitive Emails
  • > Encrypting Financial Documents
  • > Secure Chatting

PII-Sentry

Point Sentry at your storage endpoint. It streams metadata to identify PII clusters without moving data, ensuring zero impact on production performance.

  • > Finding Misplaced Sensitive Documents
  • > Monthly Compliance Audits
  • > Email Scans For Sensitive Data Leak

Air Bridge

Initialize the one-way tunnel. The system chunks data and verifies checksums at every 'save point,' allowing transfers to resume instantly after any network drop.

  • > Moving Data To Cloud
  • > Moving To A Different Database
  • > Secure Device To Device Data Transfer
